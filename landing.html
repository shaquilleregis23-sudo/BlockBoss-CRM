Features

Everything your team needs in the field

One app for your reps and managers. No training required — it works like a map with a sales layer on top.

🗺
Map-Based Canvassing
Color-coded pins by status. Navigate leads right from the map. Filter by sun score, disposition, or rep assignment.
🏠
NYC Homeowner Data
Load PLUTO homeowner names, property class, year built, and solar potential score for any Queens or Brooklyn neighborhood — instantly.
📵
Offline Mode
Knock doors and save dispositions even without service. Leads sync automatically when you're back online. No data lost.
📍
Live Rep GPS
See exactly where your reps are on the map in real time. Know who's in the field and who needs follow-up.
☀️
Sun Score Algorithm
Each home gets a solar potential score based on roof type, sun exposure, and existing solar status. Work the best homes first.
🔔
Callback Notifications
Set a callback time at the door. Get a push notification when it's time to follow up — even if the app is closed.
👥
Team Management
Invite reps by email, assign territories, track daily production, and see a live leaderboard from your manager dashboard.
📊
Daily Digest Email
Get an end-of-day email with knocks, appointments set, callbacks, and your top performers — automatically, every evening.
🚗
Route Optimization
One tap to optimize your knocking route across all leads in the current map view. Save drive time, knock more doors.
