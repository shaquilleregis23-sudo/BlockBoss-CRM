FAQ

Common Questions from NYC Solar Teams

Does M2 work for Long Island and New Jersey too?

Yes. While the PLUTO data is strongest for NYC's five boroughs, M2 works anywhere in the country. Reps can manually add leads in any ZIP code, and you can import CSV lists from any data source.

How current is the homeowner data?

M2 pulls from NYC's PLUTO (Primary Land Use Tax Lot Output) dataset, which is updated by the city regularly. It's the most accurate public record of NYC property ownership available.

Can I run solar and HVAC teams in the same account?

Absolutely. M2 has qualification fields for both solar (roof type, sun score, electric bill) and HVAC (heating type, gas bill, equipment age). Most NYC teams run both products and use the same map.

How many reps can I add?

The Solo plan supports 1 rep ($49/mo). Team supports up to 5 reps ($149/mo). Agency supports unlimited reps ($349/mo) with multi-territory management and white-label branding.

Does it work without internet in NYC subway dead zones?

Yes. M2 is a Progressive Web App (PWA) with full offline support. Reps can knock doors, mark dispositions, and take notes even without a signal. Everything syncs when they reconnect.
