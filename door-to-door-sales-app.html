FAQ

Door-to-Door Sales App — Common Questions

What is the best door-to-door sales app for NYC?
BlockBoss CRM is the only D2D app built specifically for NYC — it loads real homeowner names from the city's PLUTO database, works offline in low-signal neighborhoods, and installs on any iPhone or Android as a PWA without an app store download.
Does the door-to-door sales app work offline?
Yes. BlockBoss CRM is a Progressive Web App with full offline support. Reps can knock doors, mark dispositions (Interested, Not Home, Callback, Appointment Set), add notes, and capture photos — all without a cell signal. Data syncs automatically when the device reconnects.
How much does a door-to-door sales app cost?
BlockBoss CRM starts at $49/month for solo reps, $149/month for teams of up to 5, and $349/month for agencies with unlimited reps. All plans are flat-rate — no per-seat fees. Includes a 14-day free trial.
Can managers track reps in real time?
Yes. Team and Agency plans include live GPS tracking — managers see every active rep as a colored dot on the map, updating in real time. The Manager Dashboard (manager.html) also shows doors knocked, appointments set, and callback compliance per rep every day.
What phones does it work on?
Any modern iPhone or Android smartphone. BlockBoss CRM is a Progressive Web App — reps open it in their mobile browser and add it to their home screen in two taps. No App Store or Google Play required.
