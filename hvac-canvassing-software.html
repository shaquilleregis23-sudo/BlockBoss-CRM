FAQ

HVAC Canvassing Software — Common Questions

What is HVAC canvassing software?
HVAC canvassing software is a field sales tool that helps door-to-door HVAC sales reps manage leads, qualify homeowners (heating type, gas bill, system age), set appointments, and report back to managers — all from a mobile device. BlockBoss CRM is built specifically for this workflow.
Can reps qualify HVAC leads at the door?
Yes. Each lead card in BlockBoss CRM includes HVAC-specific qualification fields: heating type (gas/oil/electric), current gas bill, HVAC equipment age, and upgrade interest. Reps fill these out while still at the door — no paper forms or callbacks to the office.
Does BlockBoss CRM handle both HVAC and solar in one account?
Yes. Most NYC teams sell both. BlockBoss CRM includes qualification fields for solar (roof type, sun score, electric bill) and HVAC (heating type, gas bill, system age) in the same lead card, so one rep can assess a home for both products on a single visit.
Does HVAC canvassing software work offline?
Yes. BlockBoss CRM works fully offline — essential for NYC neighborhoods with spotty signal. Reps mark dispositions, qualify leads, and set appointments without connectivity. Everything syncs when they reconnect to WiFi or LTE.
How is BlockBoss different from generic CRMs for HVAC sales?
Generic CRMs like Salesforce or HubSpot are built for inside sales. BlockBoss CRM is built for the front porch — big one-tap disposition buttons, NYC homeowner data on the map, offline mode, HVAC qualification fields, appointment SMS confirmations, and flat-rate pricing with no per-seat fees.
